P-N6 Avoid the use of conjunctions and or words with ambiguous meanings

Avoid names with ``And'', ``Or'', ``Other'', ``Part'', ``Type'', ``Category'', ``Entity'' and those related to datatypes like ``Date'' or ``String''.
